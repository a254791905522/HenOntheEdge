Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Hen On The Edge ("the Game", "we", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we do or do not collect when you play our arcade game and how we handle it. By using Hen On The Edge, you agree to the terms described here.

Information We Do Not Collect

Hen On The Edge does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. We do not gather:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or physical address
  • Device ID, advertising ID, or IP address
  • Location or geolocation data
  • Gameplay data such as scores, levels, or session length
  • Any other data that could identify you or your device

Data Stored on Your Device

Progress, settings, and preferences (for example, sound options or unlocked levels) may be saved locally on your device. This data remains on your device only and is not sent to our servers or any third party. You can remove it by uninstalling the Game.

Third-Party Services

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social plug-ins that would collect your data. If the Game or this website links to other sites (such as the App Store), those sites have their own privacy practices, which we do not control.

Children and Families

Hen On The Edge is suitable for players aged 4 and over. Because we do not collect personal information, we do not knowingly collect data from children. Our practices are consistent with applicable laws regarding children's privacy, including COPPA and GDPR where relevant.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We encourage you to review this page periodically. Your continued use of the Game after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.

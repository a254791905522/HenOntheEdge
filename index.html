Hen On The Edge

Keep the Hen on the Path

🆓 Free 🎮 Arcade 📱 iPhone & iPad 👶 Ages 4+

Keep the hen safely on the path. Hen On The Edge is a fun and challenging arcade game where your goal is to keep the hen on the path and away from the edge. The path constantly changes shape—square, rectangle, and other tricky layouts—making it harder to stay on track. Control the hen carefully and maintain balance as the challenge increases.

Why You'll Love It

  • Dodge the Edge: The path shifts and transforms. Stay focused and keep the hen from falling off.
  • Timed Challenges: Survive for a set number of seconds while staying within the path to clear each level.
  • Power-Ups: Use temporary obstacle control, level skip, and other boosts to make gameplay more strategic and dynamic.
  • Test Your Reflexes: Quick reactions and steady control are key to keeping the hen on the path.
  • Increasing Difficulty: Levels get trickier with new path shapes and objectives—see how long you can last.
  • Play Anywhere: Available on iPhone and iPad. Perfect for short sessions or long runs on the go.
